CORNIFIED INSTANT PORRIDGE

Cornified Instant Porridge appeals to families that are looking for breakfast that is convinient, nutritious and tasty. Cornified Instant Porridge is available in a range of five flavours in 1kg amd 2kg pack size. It is smooth and creamy with a flavourful aroma. Ready to eat in minutes-by just adding warm milk or water - Cornified Instant Porridge can also be enjoyed as an 'any-time-of-the-day' snack by increasing the amount of water or milk, which makes an excellent filling smoothie.

Available Flavours: Strawberry, Vanilla, Banana, Original and Chocolate

Available sizes: 1kg and 2kg
